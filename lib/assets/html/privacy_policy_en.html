Privacy Policy for SportsMagicBox

Effective Date: January 29, 2025

At SportsMagicBox, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No Personal Data: SportsMagicBox does not collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or payment details since no login, sign-up, or transactions are required.
  • No User Accounts: We do not store any user credentials or profile information.

2. Information We May Collect

To provide functionality and enhance app performance, we may collect:

  • Device Information: Non-identifiable information such as device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage data to improve app features. Examples include screen views, session duration, and crash reports.

3. How We Use Collected Data

  • To ensure compatibility and improve app performance.
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and enhance the user experience.

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

SportsMagicBox integrates the following third-party services, which may process data according to their privacy policies:

  • API-Football: Used to fetch live scores, match results, and league standings. Data is retrieved in real-time and not stored by us. Read their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase Analytics: Helps us understand app performance and optimize user experience. Firebase does not collect personal data. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: Displays ads within the app. Google AdMob may use anonymous identifiers to personalize ads. Google Privacy Policy.
  • AppsFlyer: Used for app analytics and marketing attribution. AppsFlyer helps us understand app installs and engagement. AppsFlyer Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

SportsMagicBox does not collect or store personal data. Any analytics data is anonymized and processed according to third-party policies.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect app data. However, no online service is 100% secure. We recommend users take precautions when using mobile applications.

7. Children's Privacy

SportsMagicBox is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If we become aware that data from a child under 13 has been collected, we will promptly delete it.

8. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected, users do not need to request data access, deletion, or corrections. For inquiries about app functionality or this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available within the app. Continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
